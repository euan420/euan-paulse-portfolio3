About

I'm a Community & Marketing Manager with 4+ years of experience building, supporting, and strengthening communities across the Web3 ecosystem.

I entered Web3 at 17, built my expertise from the ground up, and progressed into leadership roles through consistent performance. Since 2022, I've worked on 25+ Web3 projects across Solana NFT and DeFi, contributing through community management, outreach & partnerships development, moderation and social media campaigns.

I hold a BCom in Marketing Management, allowing me to combine strategic marketing principles with hands-on community execution.

One of my most significant accomplishments was serving as Global Moderator and Live Events Host for Reign of Titans, Fortune 500-backed Web3 gaming startup by AB InBev / Anheuser-Busch, from February 2024 to January 2026.

I excel at leading communities from the front. Focusing on creating safe, engaging, and well-managed spaces that support both user experience and project growth.
